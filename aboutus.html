Jesse Dills

Hi! Gregarious and affable are common terms that others have used to describe the nature of myself. I was born and raised in the rural Pacific Northwest, and I have a kindred connection to the great outdoors. I live in Virginia with my wife Cara, where we've made a home for each other. Jessica Parker
